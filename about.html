Focus on Form

Banyon Street was born out of a desire for uncompromising quality in everyday wear. Frustrated by the fast-fashion cycle and the absence of beautifully constructed fundamentals, our founders set out to create a uniform for the modern minimalist.

Every piece is designed in-house with a relentless focus on silhouette, fabric weight, and subtle detailing. We source premium heavyweights and vintage washes to give our garments character that only improves with time.
